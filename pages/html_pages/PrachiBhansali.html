About Prachi Bhansali

Google career:

Prachi has been at Google since Mar, 2017 (4 yrs 3 months). Her current role is as a Software Engineer. Prachi played a role in the 2021 Summer Tech Exchange program as a Project Mentor for team Fedora in the Software Development Studio course.

Prachi's favorites:

All the teams I have worked at, have given me the opportunity to work on infrastructure problems, API layer problems, customer facing issues and many more". Her favorite college course is Database Systems because she loved learning about the intricacies of databases and how they store millions on records, still keeping it accessible within reasonable time. Her favorite color is yellow and her favorite food is pizza.

One skill learned during the pandemic:

Prachi states, "Learning from documentation and updating missing documentation for new members on the team. This is a very undervalued skill - good documentation can take up time when you start writing it but it makes life of everyone on the team significantly better in the long term".

Prachi's advice for future Tech professionals:

  • Reason with data, always. Any argument becomes twice as rich if presented with data.
  • Learn deeply by asking "why", understand the status quo and challenge it with data if it does not seem right
